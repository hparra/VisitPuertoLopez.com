What to explore in Puerto Lopez

Puerto Lopez is a traditional fishing town on the central coast of Ecuador famous for humpback whale watching, the Machalilla National Park, and the poor man's alternative to the Galapagos, Isla de la Plata. An assortment of trails, forests, beaches and reefs await the curious traveller. The Puerto Lopez beach is lined with various huts to grab a drink, and local & international cuisine provide meals at any time of day. When you're ready to relax choose among a variety of hotels & hostels. There is something for everyone, so come on down!

What should I do in Puerto Lopez?

Explore

Puerto Lopez is the spot to launch adventures throughout the Ecuadorian coast, whether it's treks, diving, or museums.

Eat

Where there are fisherman there is seafood. Many restaurants serve traditional coastal dishes while foreigners who have settled here have brought along their cuisine. There is plenty to eat here!

Sleep

After longs days of adventure you need a place to relax. Puerto Lopez has something for every type of budget.
